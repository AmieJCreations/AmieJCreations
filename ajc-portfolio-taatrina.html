Madame Taa'Trina

This is Madame Taa'Trina, the Voodoo Priestess. She roams the savage swamps where she lives searching for ingredients for her potions. She is pictured here getting ready to brew a potion in her trusty human skull cauldron. Now the question is "How did she come across such a cauldron?". This painting was made during the 2018 Drawtober Drawing Challenge Day 2 - "Frog Witch".

(Original painting - 8x10 inches)
