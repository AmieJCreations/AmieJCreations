Natarie and Peki

The Giant Crab Hunters

This is the beach elf Natarie and her eel companion Peki. They are the mighty giant crab hunters for their tribe. A little bit of back story on the beach elves and their eel companions... My idea is that when the elves start coming of age their hair and eyes start changing colors and what ever the colors are they pair off with an eel that has the same. They in turn find each other like their souls become paired and they can sense it to find one another. They become bonded companions and are never separated...if one dies the other dies.

(Original painting - 8x10 inches)
